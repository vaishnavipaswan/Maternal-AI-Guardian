Pregnancy Risk Timeline

Week 1-4: Initial pregnancy symptoms. Monitor nausea and fatigue levels.
Week 5-8: Critical organ development. Avoid alcohol and maintain folic acid intake.
Week 9-12: First trimester screening. Monitor blood pressure and weight gain.
Week 13-16: Second trimester begins. Energy levels typically improve.
Week 17-20: Anatomy scan period. Monitor fetal movements.
Week 21-24: Viability milestone. Watch for preterm labor signs.
Week 25-28: Glucose screening. Monitor for gestational diabetes.
Week 29-32: Third trimester monitoring. Watch blood pressure closely.
Week 33-36: Preparation for delivery. Monitor for preeclampsia signs.
Week 37-40: Full term. Ready for delivery. Watch for labor signs.
